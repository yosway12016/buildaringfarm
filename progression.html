🌱 Early Game

Build a strong foundation with starter seeds, upgrades, and early income.

🌿
Best starter seeds
Choose the right early seeds to boost income and unlock stronger options fast.
  • Prioritize high value starter seeds for quick cash.
  • Upgrade the first seed to level 20 quickly.
  • Use early money to unlock better plots and tools.
  • Keep seed purchases efficient to avoid overspending.

First upgrades
Invest in the upgrades that matter most during the first few ring cycles.
  • Upgrade plot level to unlock more planting space.

  • Avoid spending on expensive late-game seeds too soon.
💰
Beginner money strategy
Build a steady cash flow before jumping into higher-cost upgrades.
  • Keep enough reserves for key upgrades.
  • Reinvest early earnings into seed and ring upgrades.
  • Buy only what increases income reliably.
  • Use cheap seeds to fuel the first growth cycles.
🚫
Avoid wasting money
Stay efficient with every early game purchase.
  • Don't overspend on rare seeds too early.
  • Skip upgrades that don't improve income yet.
  • Keep your plot upgrades aligned with seed output.
  • Hold money until the next high-value upgrade is available.

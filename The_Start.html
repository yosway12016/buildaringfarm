🌱 The Start

Beginner progression guide for Build A Ring Farm

🚀 Starting Guide

Use Code: 2KLIKES
1. Claim the free seed from the code.
2. Upgrade your plot to Level 3.
3. Place the free seed and upgrade it to Level 20.
4. Upgrade Saw Yield and Sprinkler Power to Level 20-25.
5. Save money and buy Tomato Seeds.
6. Fill the outer ring with Tomatoes and upgrade them to Level 20.
7. Buy Prismatic or Divine seeds like Golden Apple.
8. Continue until you can afford Exotic Seeds like Sunflower & Pepper.

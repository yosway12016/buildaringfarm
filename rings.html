🪚 Saw Yield

Upgrading your saw increases your yield multiplier. Each level gives you +2x to your base multiplier. This stacks with your ring multiplier and mutation multiplier for massive income gains!

Formula: Base Income × Ring Multiplier × Saw Multiplier × Mutation Multiplier = Total Income

💦 Sprinkler Power

Sprinklers automatically water your plants, increasing their growth speed and income. Higher level sprinklers cover more plots and provide better bonuses. Upgrade your sprinkler in the Gear Shop to maximize passive income!

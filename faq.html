❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Build A Ring Farm

🌽 How many total seeds are there?

There are currently 39 unique seeds available in the game.

✨ What are the different seed rarities?

Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Secret, Prismatic, Divine, and Exotic.

🧬 What are Seed Mutations?

  • Wet = 1.35x
  • Frozen = 1.75x
  • Void = 2.25x
  • Radioactive = 3x
  • Rainbow = 5x

🌪️ What are Mutation Events?

Mutation Events are weather events that trigger mutation chances.

  • Each event has 1–3 rolls.
  • Every placed plant gets a roll chance.

Example: If you have 20 plants placed and a Black Hole event occurs, every plant gets a 3% chance to mutate into Void.

💧 What does the Sprinkler upgrade do?

The Sprinkler reduces the amount of time each plant stage takes to grow depending on the sprinkler power.

Example: If a plant stage normally takes 30 seconds, the sprinkler removes a portion of that time from every stage after being sprinkled.

🪚 What does Saw Yield do?

Saw Yield increases the number of units harvested per triangle.

💍 What are Ring Multipliers?

  • Base Ring = 7x
  • Middle Ring = 13x
  • Outer Ring = 19x

💰 What Money Multipliers exist?

Available multipliers: 2x, 4x, 6x, 8x, 10x, and 12x

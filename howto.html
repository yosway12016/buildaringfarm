Understanding upgrade charts and money calculations

📊 Reading the Upgrade Chart

This chart shows the upgrade costs on the left side and base money-per-unit values on the right side for Void Fruit from level 1 to level 100.

The values shown are before any personal multipliers are applied. So if you have a game-pass, you'll need to multiply the chart's money-per-unit value by your own multiplier to get your actual per-unit value. If your fruit has a mutation, multiply by that mutation value as well.

Example:

If the chart says a fruit makes 1M, and you have a 4x multiplier, then your actual income would be:

1M × 4 = 4M

If that same fruit also has a 3x mutation, then it would be:

1M × 4 × 3 = 12M

Each section for both Money Per Unit and Upgrade Prices is laid out in groups of 20 levels. Milestone levels are marked in parentheses every 5 levels for easier reading.

Everything else should be mostly self-explanatory, but I'm also the one who made the chart, so if you need help reading anything, feel free to ask me! ^.^
